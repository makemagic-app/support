Privacy Policy

for Dream Weaver Within

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Dream Weaver Within ("we," "us," "our") is a dream journaling and interpretation app published by Make Today Magical. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — your email address and password (handled securely by our authentication provider) when you create an account.
  • Dream entries — the dream titles and descriptions you write or dictate, along with any interpretations generated from them.
  • Subscription information — purchase and entitlement data (such as whether you have an active subscription) managed through our payment provider, RevenueCat, and the App Store.
  • Basic usage data — general technical information such as app crashes or performance data, used only to keep the app running smoothly.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and dream journal
  • To generate dream interpretations you request
  • To manage your subscription and restore purchases across devices
  • To provide support when you contact us
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the app

We do not sell your personal information or dream entries to third parties, and we do not use your dream content for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Dream Weaver Within relies on a small number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — securely stores your account details and dream entries.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription purchases and entitlements.
  • AI interpretation service — processes the text of your dream to generate an interpretation. Your dream text is sent securely for this purpose and is not used to identify you personally.

All data sent between the app and these services is protected using standard industry encryption (HTTPS/TLS) while in transit.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored on secure servers with access restrictions in place to protect it from unauthorised access. While we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can edit or delete individual dream entries at any time within the app.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

Children's Privacy

Dream Weaver Within is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
