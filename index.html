Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Open Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone, select Dream Weaver Within, and choose Cancel Subscription.

I didn't get my free trial — what happened?

Free trials are only available once per Apple ID. If you've previously subscribed or trialled the app, a new trial period won't be offered on the same account.

My dreams didn't save — can I get them back?

Dreams are saved to your account automatically when you're signed in. If something looks missing, email us with your account details and we'll help track it down.

Can I use the app on more than one device?

Yes — sign in with the same account on any device to access your dream journal and interpretations.
